# Pricing — Perth Concrete Care (Perth, WA)

All prices in AUD per square metre, supply and install, residential guide rates.
Final pricing confirmed after a free on-site inspection. Larger areas achieve a
lower per-m² rate; small or heavily detailed jobs price higher per m².
Service area: Perth north of the river (Butler to Perth CBD).
Quotes: free, fixed-price, written. Phone 0448 483 226 or
sales@perthconcretecare.com.au.

## Epoxy Flake Floors
- Price: $85–$100/m²
- Includes: moisture-tolerant primer, flake broadcast (full, partial or sparse), UV/oil/chemical-resistant polyaspartic topcoat
- Best for: garages, workshops, patios, pool surrounds

## Metallic Epoxy Floors
- Price: $160–$180/m²
- Includes: 100% solids metallic epoxy, custom blends/highlights/3D effects, UV-stable polyurethane topcoat
- Best for: garages, showrooms, living areas

## Hone & Seal / Honed Concrete (Outdoor)
- Price: $60–$80/m²
- Includes: precision diamond-honed finish, UV-stable protective sealer, exterior-grade slip resistance (P3–P5 options)
- Best for: alfresco areas, pool surrounds, paths, smoothing rough exposed aggregate

## Polished Concrete (Indoor)
- Price: $140–$220/m²
- Includes: full mechanical polish (400–3000 grit), choice of cream / salt & pepper / full exposure, satin to mirror gloss options
- Best for: indoor living areas, showrooms, commercial interiors

## Price factors
Total floor area, slab hardness, site access, existing coatings or
contaminants, crack and surface repairs, moisture conditions, and edge/detail
work all influence the final per-m² rate.
